ACM - Association of Computing Machinery

ACM is widely recognized as the premier membership organization for computing professionals, delivering resources that advance computing as a science and a profession; enable professional development; and promote policies and research that benefit society. ACM hosts the computing industry's leading Digital Library, and serves its global members and the computing profession with journals and magazines, conferences, workshops, electronic forums, and Learning Center.

ACM Student Chapter

UEM Jaipur ACM Student Chapter provides diverse opportunities for students to engage in computing-related activities. In addition to organizing talks, workshops, and career sessions, our chapter hosts a series of events throughout the year. Recent highlights include a bootcamp for coding challenges in Sept'23 & Oct'23, with over 70 participants, and ACM DSP session in Sept'23 and 2 DSP in Mar'24, attracting 200+ attendees. We also conduct coding competitions regularly, with notable participation in Feb'24 and Mar'24, involving 100+ participants. Flagship events like the 'HackUEM' mini-internal-hackathon and 'AceHack 3.0', national-level hackathon have garnered significant interest, anticipating over 500 innovators from 30+ cities. Check out AceHack website at acehack.uem.edu.in for more details! Future plans include workshops, coding competitions, DSP sessions, and a hackathon, demonstrating our commitment to providing enriching experiences.

University of Engineering and Management

UEM Jaipur, established in 2012, stands as a premier state private university. With an impressive highest placement offer of Rs 72 lakhs per annum and an average of Rs 6.5 lakhs, we prioritize academic excellence. Ranked 1st in North India by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India, our commitment to innovation is evident through our recognition as a mentor institute by AICTE. In just a decade, we've become a global leader, offering specialized programs in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and more. Our Physiotherapy department holds a 4-star rating from the Indian Association of Physiotherapists. Throughout the pandemic, we maintained academic continuity, ensuring students graduated on time. Our campus spans 32 acres, featuring modern facilities, and our alumni are excelling in top companies worldwide, showcasing the success of UEM Jaipur’s education and placement initiatives.
