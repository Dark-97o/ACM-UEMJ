ACM

ACM is widely recognized as the premier membership organization for computing professionals, delivering resources that advance computing as a science and a profession; enable professional development; and promote policies and research that benefit society. ACM hosts the computing industry's leading Digital Library, and serves its global members and the computing profession with journals and magazines, conferences, workshops, electronic forums, and Learning Center.

ACM Student Chapter

The UEM Jaipur ACM Student Chapter actively engages students through a wide range of technical events and learning opportunities throughout the year. Our key activities include:

  • AceHack 4.0: A flagship 36-hour hackathon with 5000+ registrations and 800+ in-person hackers from 50+ cities.
  • HackUEM 4.0: A 12-hour blockchain hackathon powered by Algorand, featuring 350+ participants.
  • Stacks Fullstack Bootcamp: Hands-on fullstack learning experience with 150+ learners.
  • Logic Ladder Coding Competition: Conducted during TechUtopia 2025, attracting strong participation.
  • Git & GitHub Workshops: High-engagement sessions with 250+ students learning essential version control skills.
  • Distinguished Speaker Sessions: Talks by Prof. Dr. Tanmoy Chakraborty (IIT Delhi) and Prof. Dr. Sanjay Mishra, each attended by 200+ students.
  • Flutter & Machine Learning Workshop: Practical training led by industry experts with internship opportunities for learners.

The chapter continues to expand its initiatives with upcoming workshops, hackathons, and technical sessions, ensuring meaningful and industry-relevant learning experiences for all students.

UEM Jaipur

UEM Jaipur, established in 2012, is recognized as a premier state private university known for its strong academic standards and industry-oriented education. Key highlights include:

  • Top Placements: Highest package of Rs 72 LPA and an average package of Rs 6.5 LPA.
  • National Ranking: Ranked 1st in North India by the Ministry of Education, Government of India.
  • AICTE Recognition: Identified as a mentor institute for driving innovation and quality education.
  • Specialized Programs: Advanced courses in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and emerging technologies.
  • 4-Star Physiotherapy Department: Rated by the Indian Association of Physiotherapists for excellence in academics and clinical training.
  • Pandemic Academic Continuity: Ensured uninterrupted learning, allowing students to graduate on time.
  • Modern Campus: A 32-acre campus with state-of-the-art facilities and a strong innovation ecosystem.
  • Global Alumni Presence: Graduates working in top companies worldwide, reflecting the university’s strong placement performance.

UEM Jaipur continues to uphold its commitment to quality education, innovation, and student success across all disciplines.
